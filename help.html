📊 XRP Insights Dashboard

Your comprehensive XRP market intelligence platform

🎯 Overview

XRP Insights Dashboard is a free, real-time analytics platform for tracking XRP ETF holdings, exchange balances, market sentiment, and more. All data is sourced from official APIs and updated regularly.

📈 Real-Time Data

Live price updates, ETF holdings, and exchange balances refreshed every few minutes.

🌐 Multi-Language

Full support for English and Korean languages.

📱 Mobile Ready

Responsive design works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

🤖 AI Powered

AI-generated market insights and analysis using Claude AI.

📑 Dashboard Tabs

📈 Trading Tab

View XRP Spot ETF trading data including daily volume, price, and performance metrics for all US-listed XRP ETFs like GXRP (Grayscale), XRP (Bitwise), XRPC (Canary), and more.

💎 Holdings Tab

Track the total XRP holdings across all spot ETFs. See which ETF providers hold the most XRP and monitor daily inflows/outflows with interactive charts.

🏦 Exchange Tab

Monitor XRP balances across 40+ major cryptocurrency exchanges. Compare current balances to historical data to identify accumulation or distribution patterns.

  • Fetch Live Data - Pulls real-time balances from XRPL
  • Historical Comparison - Compare to Feb 24, 2025 baseline
  • Net Flow Analysis - See which exchanges are gaining/losing XRP

📊 Analytics Tab

Deep dive into exchange analytics with charts, rankings, and flow analysis.

  • Exchange Flow Analysis NEW - Visual breakdown of inflows vs outflows
  • Top Rankings - See top exchanges by holdings
  • Export Data - Download as CSV or JSON

🏆 Top 10K Tab

Explore the XRP rich list - the top 10,000 largest XRP wallets on the XRPL.

  • Whale Alerts NEW - Live large transaction alerts
  • Filter by size - View Top 25, 50, 100, or 500 wallets
  • Wallet analysis - See balance distribution and dominance

📰 News Tab

Stay updated with the latest XRP and crypto news from multiple sources.

  • AI News Summary NEW - AI-generated daily digest
  • Sentiment Analysis - See bullish/bearish ratings
  • Multiple Sources - News from CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, and more

🤖 AI Insights Tab

AI-powered market analysis and portfolio tools.

  • Technical Analysis NEW - RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands
  • Portfolio Tracker - Calculate your holdings value and P&L
  • XRPL Live Data - Real-time ledger statistics
  • Price Scenarios - See potential portfolio values at different prices

𝕏 Social Tab

Track XRP community sentiment and social metrics.

  • Sentiment Score - Community sentiment gauge (0-100)
  • Twitter/Reddit Stats - Follower counts and activity
  • Trending Topics - Popular XRP hashtags

✨ New Features

🐋 Whale Alerts

Real-time alerts for large XRP transactions (1M+ XRP). Filter by deposits, withdrawals, or transaction size. Links directly to XRPScan for transaction details.

📊 Technical Analysis

Professional-grade technical indicators calculated from 30-day price history:

  • RSI (14-day) - Relative Strength Index with overbought/oversold signals
  • MACD (12, 26, 9) - Moving Average Convergence Divergence with crossover alerts
  • Bollinger Bands (20, 2) - Price volatility bands with position indicator

💰 Exchange Flow Analysis

Visual analysis of XRP flows across exchanges. Net outflows typically indicate bullish sentiment (less selling pressure), while net inflows may indicate bearish sentiment.

🤖 AI News Summarizer

AI-powered daily digest of top XRP news headlines with sentiment analysis. Click "Generate" to create a fresh summary.

❓ FAQ

Why is data not loading?

The backend server may be waking up (free tier has cold starts). Wait 30-60 seconds and refresh. If issues persist, check your internet connection.

How often is data updated?

ETF data: Every 5 minutes. Exchange data: On-demand when you click "Fetch Live Data". News: Every 5 minutes.

Is this financial advice?

No. This dashboard is for informational and educational purposes only. Always consult a financial advisor before making investment decisions.
