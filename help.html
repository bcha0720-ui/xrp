ETF Trading Tab

Track real-time trading volume and price data for all XRP-related ETFs.

ETF Categories

Spot ETFs ETFs that hold actual XRP (GXRP, XRP, XRPC, XRPZ, TOXR, XRPR). These directly track XRP price.
Futures ETFs ETFs using XRP futures contracts (UXRP, XRPI, XRPM). May include leveraged products.
Index ETFs Multi-crypto ETFs with XRP exposure (EZPZ, GDLC, BITW). XRP is one of multiple holdings.
Canada ETFs Canadian-listed XRP ETFs (XRP.TO, XRPP.TO). Trade on Toronto Stock Exchange.

Time Periods

Switch between different time periods to analyze trading patterns:

  • Daily - Today's trading volume
  • Weekly - Last 5 trading days
  • Monthly - Last 30 days
  • Yearly - Last 12 months
USE CASE

Analyzing Institutional Interest

  • High volume in Spot ETFs indicates strong institutional buying/selling
  • Compare daily vs weekly volume to spot unusual activity
  • Rising volume with rising price = bullish confirmation
  • Rising volume with falling price = potential capitulation

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?
ETF data updates every 5 minutes during market hours. Exchange and Top 10K data is cached for 1-24 hours but can be manually refreshed. Price data updates in real-time from CoinGecko.
Where does the data come from?
ETF data comes from Yahoo Finance. Exchange and rich list data comes directly from the XRP Ledger via XRPScan API. Burn data is calculated from ledger information.
Why do some ETFs show no data?
Some ETFs may be newly launched, not trading yet, or temporarily unavailable. The dashboard shows "‚Äî" for unavailable data rather than incorrect numbers.
What does "whale" mean?
A whale is a wallet holding 1 million or more XRP. These large holders can significantly impact the market when they buy or sell.
How accurate is the exchange wallet data?
We track known exchange wallets that have been publicly identified. Exchanges may have additional unlabeled wallets, so totals represent a minimum estimate.
Can I export the data?
Yes! The Exchange Analytics tab has CSV and JSON export buttons. The AI Insights tab can generate PDF reports.
Is this financial advice?
No. This dashboard provides data and information for educational purposes. Always conduct your own research and consult financial advisors before making investment decisions.
